The Olympiad Match Program has been instrumental in transforming the lives of countless promising students from low-income backgrounds across the nation, empowering them to unlock billions of dollars in scholarships.


The Need

Each year, over 30,000 exceptional high school students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds possess the academic qualifications to thrive at the nation's top colleges. Sadly, more than 80% of them do not apply to any selective college, resulting in a significant underrepresentation on leading campuses.

Many of these bright individuals believe they cannot afford to attend a top college, lack essential information about the application process, or simply assume they won't be accepted. Consequently, they miss out on life-changing educational opportunities, impacting their future trajectories. Some may never reach their full academic potential, while others may not pursue higher education at all.

This widespread underutilization of talent represents a profound loss in human potential. A diverse and educated society is crucial for addressing global challenges, making it imperative to support promising students from all economic backgrounds.


Proven Success

Through innovative data-driven approaches, Olympiad Match identifies and supports thousands of high-achieving, low-income students, enabling them to realize their academic aspirations, including attending top-tier colleges on full scholarships. Our comprehensive programs provide ongoing resources and foster a supportive community of Scholars, ensuring their success in college and beyond.

We are committed to continual improvement, striving to empower the next generation of high-achieving students from low-income backgrounds to excel academically, contribute meaningfully to their communities, and succeed in their careers.
