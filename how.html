Application Components

Registration is currently closed. We are reaching out to counselors for the finalists of the class of 2024. Registration for the class of 2025 will open in December 2024.


To be considered for a full four-year scholarship to our college partners, your first step is to submit a free, online Olympiad Match application, which will serve as your application to our college partners if you are selected as a Finalist.


  • Information on your academics, activities, household, and financial background*
  • Two essays and short answers
  • Personal Statment of 650 words
  • Two teacher recommendations
  • If you are selected as a finalist, an official request for a School Report, transcript and additional transcripts (unofficial transcripts are accepted) will be sent to your current high school counselor
  • School Profile (optional, but recommended)
  • Any available standardized test score reports (if taken). We accept SAT, ACT, Subject Tests, IB, AP, or TOEFL test scores (unofficial test score reports are accepted)
