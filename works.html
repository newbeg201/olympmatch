The Olympiad Match Program aims to connect low-income students aspiring to pursue bachelor's degrees in the United States with suitable universities. A key requirement for participation is involvement in at least one international Olympiad during high school, supported by an official statement confirming participation.


This program streamlines the application process akin to the Common App, condensing multiple applications into a single, comprehensive form. Upon selection as a finalist, we liaise with your counselor to obtain your academic report and official transcripts.


In the final stage, we endeavor to match you with a university that aligns with your personality and aspirations. Subsequently, the university may request additional requirements to secure your final acceptance.

Olympiad Match Application Steps

  1. Obtain your official statement of participation in an international Olympiad from the competition, whether you participated as a leader or a participant.
  2. When registration opens, apply for online training to familiarize yourself with the Olympiad Match program's application process. This training provides an overview of how to apply effectively.
  3. Apply to universities/colleges, ranking them in order of your personal preference. Submit applications for early and regular admissions, adhering to the deadlines specified on each university's official website.
  4. Upon application, universities may request additional requirements from you personally. Fulfill these requirements in accordance with each university's guidelines to complete your evaluation.
  5. If selected as a finalist, we will contact your counselor to obtain your academic report and official transcripts, ensuring your eligibility for the program.
  6. Upon receiving an official acceptance statement from the university that selected you, follow additional steps outlined on the university portal. These steps may include confirming your enrollment, submitting financial aid documents, or completing housing forms.
  7. Join the community of Olympiad Match students at your chosen college. Engage with fellow students to share experiences and support one another as you navigate your academic journey.
