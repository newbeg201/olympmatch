Main Eligibility Criteria

    Please Check the eligibility webpage for more details


  • Must be a high school senior
  • Must have participated in an international Olympiad such as IMO, IBO, EGOI, IPHO, IOI, etc., and possess a certificate of participation and an official letter from the respective Olympiad confirming participation
  • Must come from a low-income family with an annual salary less than $55,000, with minimal assets, and often qualify for their school's free or reduced-price meals
  • Must exhibit strong character traits, including resilience, integrity, and motivation to succeed
