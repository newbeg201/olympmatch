Students frequently discover the Olympiad Match Program through the guidance and support of their school counselors or advisors. Our aim is to provide you with the tools and resources necessary to assist your students in preparing for our application process.


Assisting Your Students

Counselors play a crucial role in guiding students as they plan for their futures and navigate the college application process. Our Student Resource Center offers a plethora of resources tailored specifically to aid high-achieving, low-income students in realizing their full potential. These resources include:


  • Application Overview: The Olympiad Match Program for High School Seniors
  • Application Instructions: Our Olympiad Match Program Application Instructions offer a comprehensive overview of the required components. This entails a complete college application, and we encourage students to commence their preparations early to ensure they can submit their strongest possible application.

  • Counselor and Advisor FAQ: Here are answers to common questions from counselors:
    • Do I need to submit a transcript for my student? No, if the student is selected as a finalist, the principal will receive an official email requesting the submission of the official transcript for your student. Please note that students cannot directly upload these materials to their application.
